Laboratory Supplies

We have categorized the laboratory supplies in the following manner.

Chemicals

We offer a range of essential research chemicals, including Common Solvents like Distilled water, Ethanol, Methanol, and Acetone, as well as Acids & Bases, Salts & Buffers, Reagents for Assays, and Gases. Currently, Research Made Easy (RME) acts as a facilitator for these orders. We will process your requests and connect you with authorized sellers who will handle the direct delivery of chemicals. Please note that all orders for chemicals must be placed by an authorized person and require a valid organizational license.

Equipment

We've partnered with reputable sellers and manufacturers to bring you a wide range of laboratory equipment. For more specialized needs, we also offer equipment such as PCR machines and spectrophotometers. Can't find the specific equipment you're looking for on our portal? Don't worry! Contact our team directly, and we'll do our best to source it for you and provide it within your required timeframe.

Characterization & Data Analysis

For your advanced research needs, we've established partnerships with recognized institutions and central instrumentation facilities across India. This allows us to facilitate seamless access to cutting-edge testing for your samples.

You can typically expect your samples to be tested and results available within 10 days, though this timeframe may vary depending on the current demand and slot availability at the facilities.

You have two convenient options:

  • Raw Data Access: Opt to receive the raw data directly from the testing facility.
  • Comprehensive Analysis & Ease: For an additional fee, choose our sample analysis service. Our experts will handle the data interpretation, providing you with analyzed results and allowing you to stay at ease focusing on your core research.

Scholar Desk

The Scholar Desk is your dedicated support hub designed specifically for early career researchers and independent scholars. We offer a suite of paid services to empower you at every stage of your research journey, providing expert assistance so you can focus on your groundbreaking work.

Our services include:

  • Research Paper Writing Assistance: Get expert guidance in drafting and refining your research papers.
  • Plagiarism Checks: Ensure the originality and integrity of your work with thorough plagiarism screenings.
  • Presentation Preparation: Create impactful presentations that effectively convey your research findings.
  • Poster Preparation & Printing: Design and print professional-quality research posters for conferences and events.
  • Manuscript Writing: Receive comprehensive support in developing and structuring your full manuscript.
  • Paper Formatting: Ensure your paper adheres perfectly to journal-specific guidelines and submission requirements.
  • Grammar Checks: Polish your writing with meticulous grammar and linguistic reviews.
  • Characterization Data Analysis: Gain insights from your complex data with expert analytical support.

The invaluable assistance you receive at Scholar Desk comes from well-known professors with years of extensive experience in your specific research field. They're here to share their profound knowledge and help elevate the quality and impact of your work.

Are you an institution looking to host a research-focused event on your campus? You can request our team to conduct events right at your institute! While conducting events on campus is subject to finding the right resources, the RME team is here to support you in several key ways:

Please note that while RME provides significant organizational and personnel support, the cost of the event will need to be covered by your institution.

How is RME different from other lab supply or research portals?

Research Made Easy (RME) stands apart from other portals by being a unified, end-to-end platform dedicated to simplifying the entire research ecosystem — not just a niche supplier marketplace.

While other platforms typically focus on one research domain (e.g., only chemicals, only lab rentals, or only data analysis), RME brings all core research services together in one place. We are not manufacturers ourselves, but we bridge the gap between researchers and verified suppliers — especially direct manufacturers — which helps maintain affordability, transparency, and authenticity.

A Unified Ecosystem for Research

Most platforms focus on isolated services. RME acts as a complete research assistant, offering:

  • Lab Supplies: (chemicals, equipment, consumables, electronics, and safety gear)
  • Characterization & Analysis services: through partner institutes
  • Academic Assistance: via our Scholar Desk (for writing, formatting, and plagiarism help)
  • Outreach Programs: including lab visits, guest talks, and skill-based workshops
  • Career Opportunities: in research — internships, jobs, fellowships, and more

RME isn’t just a supplier platform — it’s a research companion. We integrate sourcing, services, training, mentorship, and collaboration into a single platform, aiming to meet 70–90% of a researcher's needs in one place.
